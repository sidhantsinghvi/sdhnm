The combination of the stepper motor, motor driver, and the chosen linear axis defines
the minimum resolution for the motorized axis.

The used components are:

Linear axis: OWIS VT 45N
- Travel: 25 mm
- Spindle pitch: 0.5 mm
- setting sensitivy: 5 µm

Motor: Joy-IT NEMA17-02
- Step angle: 1,8°
- Shaft diameter: 5 mm
- Current 1.68 A
- 2 phase design

Motor Driver: TMCM2209 Wetterott Breakout
- Microstep resolution: 8, 16, 32, 64 Microsteps per Fullstep

Therfore:
Microstep resolution: 8
- Microstep angle: 0.225°
- Distance/Microstep: 0.015625 mm = 15.625 µm

Microstep resolutuin: 16
- Microstep angle: 0.1125°
- Distance/Microstep: 0.00781 mm = 7.81 µm

Microstep resolutuin: 32
- Microstep angle: 0.05625°
- Distance/Microstep: 0.003905 mm = 3.905 µm

Microstep resolutuin: 64
- Microstep angle: 0.028125°
- Distance/Microstep: 0.0019525 mm = 1.9525 µm

The setting sensitivy of the linear axis is 5 µm: a microstep resolution 
of 32 is selected.
